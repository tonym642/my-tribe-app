# My Tribe — Stories

This file powers the **Stories** feature inside the My Tribe app.

Stories are short, meaningful pieces of content designed to replace mindless social media scrolling with **daily inspiration, wisdom, and reflection**.

Stories are shown in a **social-style carousel** where each advisor can post stories that users can tap to read.

The system may show **1–3 stories per advisor per day**, rotating randomly.

Stories should be:
- Short
- Impactful
- Easy to read in under 60 seconds
- Educational or reflective

Types of stories include:
- Biography lessons
- Historical events
- Biblical insights
- Philosophical reflections
- Motivational moments
- Cultural wisdom
- Parables
- Fictional teaching stories
