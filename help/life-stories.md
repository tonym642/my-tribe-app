# Life Stories

## What It Is
Life Stories provides inspirational biographies narrated by the advisors.

## Purpose
This feature inspires and educates through real-life stories of known and unknown individuals.

## How It Works
- You open the stories section.
- Select a story or generate a random one.
- An advisor narrates the story.
- The story is presented on a single page.

## How To Use It
1. Open the stories section.
2. Choose a story or click random.
3. Read or listen to the narration.

## Key Rules
- Stories are inspirational and educational.
- Includes both famous and unknown individuals.
- Displayed as a full page, not slides.

## Example Use Cases
- Daily inspiration
- Learning from real-life experiences
- Reflecting on personal growth

## Tips
- Use regularly for mindset growth.
- Reflect on lessons from each story.

## User Experience Goal
Feels like being told a powerful, meaningful story.
