# Campfire

## What It Is
Campfire is a guided group conversation where multiple advisors and the user engage in a structured discussion led by a moderator.

## Purpose
This feature creates a natural, flowing group conversation experience where ideas are shared in a more human and engaging way.

## How It Works
- You select participants (advisors and/or yourself).
- A moderator starts the session.
- Each participant speaks in turns.
- The conversation flows naturally.
- The session ends with a smooth fade-out led by the moderator.

## How To Use It
1. Select participants.
2. Choose a topic (or let AI suggest one).
3. Start the session.
4. Follow the conversation as it unfolds.
5. Optionally contribute when prompted.

## Key Rules
- The moderator controls the flow.
- Participants speak one at a time.
- No abrupt endings — conversations fade naturally.
- The guide does not give final conclusions; the moderator handles closure.

## Example Use Cases
- Group discussion about relationships
- Exploring different perspectives on a life situation
- Listening to a dynamic conversation between advisors

## Tips
- Choose a mix of advisors for more interesting discussions.
- Let the conversation flow without interrupting too much.
- Use it more for exploration than decision-making.

## User Experience Goal
Feels like sitting in a real conversation where ideas flow naturally and organically.
