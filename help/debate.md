# Debate

## What It Is
Debate is a structured feature where advisors present opposing viewpoints on a topic or decision.

## Purpose
This feature helps you see both sides of an argument clearly, improving decision-making and critical thinking.

## How It Works
- You select a topic or question.
- Advisors are assigned different perspectives.
- Each advisor presents arguments.
- The discussion continues in a structured format.

## How To Use It
1. Enter a topic or decision.
2. Start the debate.
3. Watch advisors present their arguments.
4. Optionally ask follow-up questions.

## Key Rules
- Advisors may disagree intentionally.
- Responses are structured and focused.
- The goal is clarity, not agreement.

## Example Use Cases
- “Should I take this business risk?”
- “Is this relationship worth continuing?”
- “Is this the right career move?”

## Tips
- Use this when you feel stuck between options.
- Pay attention to reasoning, not just conclusions.

## User Experience Goal
Feels like watching a high-level discussion between experts with different viewpoints.

## Future Improvements
- User voting on winning argument
- Multi-round debates
