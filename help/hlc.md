# High Level Conversations (HLC)

## What It Is
HLC is a message-crafting tool that helps you write thoughtful, intentional messages for real-life situations — difficult conversations, important requests, or emotionally charged topics.

## Purpose
Most people default to reactive or unclear communication. HLC helps you slow down, think through what you want to say, and express it in a way that moves things forward without creating conflict.

## How It Works
- You describe your situation and who you are speaking to.
- You select your intention — what outcome you want from the message.
- The AI generates a balanced draft designed to be heard, not just sent.
- You can adjust the tone using Stronger, Softer, or Shorter options.
- Copy your final message and send it wherever you need.

## How To Use It
1. Select a situation type (Work, Relationship, Family, or Personal).
2. Enter who the message is for.
3. Describe the situation in your own words.
4. Choose your intention — what you want to accomplish.
5. Click Generate to create your draft.
6. Use the tabs (Draft, Stronger, Softer, Shorter) to explore variations.
7. Copy the version you prefer and use it.

## Key Rules
- Be honest in your situation description — the better the input, the better the output.
- The tool generates a starting point. You can and should edit before sending.
- Stronger makes the message more assertive. Softer makes it warmer. Shorter strips it down.

## Example Use Cases
- Telling a colleague their behavior is affecting the team.
- Asking a friend to respect a boundary.
- Communicating to a family member about a recurring issue.
- Requesting something from a manager without sounding demanding.

## Tips
- Use Shorter if the draft feels too long for a text or message.
- Use Softer if you are worried the message sounds too harsh.
- Use Stronger if you feel the draft is too passive for the situation.
- Review the Examples section to see what good and poor versions look like side by side.

## User Experience Goal
Feels like having a communication coach in your pocket — helping you say the right thing at the right moment.
