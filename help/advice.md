# Advice

## What It Is
Advice is the main conversation feature where you interact with your advisors. You can ask questions, share thoughts, and receive guidance from one or multiple advisors.

## Purpose
This feature acts as your personal advisory board. It helps you think better, make decisions, and gain different perspectives instantly.

## How It Works
- You enter a message or question.
- You select one or more advisors, or use @mentions.
- Only the selected advisors respond.
- The conversation continues as a thread.
- You can direct follow-ups using @mentions or @all.

## How To Use It
1. Type your message in the chat box.
2. Select advisors or use @name to direct your message.
3. Press send.
4. Read responses and continue the conversation.
5. Use @all if you want everyone to respond.

## Key Rules
- No advisors are selected by default.
- If no advisor is selected, you will be prompted to choose.
- @mentions control who responds.
- @all triggers responses from all advisors.

## Example Use Cases
- “@Emma what should I do in this situation?”
- “@all give me your opinion on this decision.”
- “@Marcus analyze this from a mindset perspective.”

## Tips
- Be specific with your questions for better answers.
- Use individual advisors for focused insights.
- Use @all for broader perspectives.

## User Experience Goal
Feels like having a personal board of advisors available at all times.

## Future Improvements
