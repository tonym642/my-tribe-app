## My Tribe

My Tribe is a personal advisory app that gives you access to a council of AI advisors — each one representing a different dimension of your life.

The idea is simple: no single perspective is enough to make a great decision. A purely financial lens misses emotional cost. A purely emotional lens can miss practical risk. Real wisdom comes from holding multiple perspectives at once.

Your Tribe is that council. It includes a Spiritual Advisor, a Mindset Advisor, an Emotional Advisor, a Health Advisor, a Relationships Advisor, and a Financial Advisor. You can ask one advisor a question, ask your whole Tribe at once, or explore ideas through debate, story, or deep reflection.

There is also a Custom Advisor — a persona you define — who speaks from the perspective of whoever most represents your ideal self. And BVM (Best Version of Me) — not an external advisor, but your own highest self. BVM is the A-level version of you: disciplined, clear, responsible, and aligned with your highest standards. It reflects back what the best version of you would think, choose, and do.

My Tribe does not make decisions for you. It helps you think better so you can make decisions you are proud of.

## Advisors

The Tribe is designed to give you multiple perspectives when thinking through questions, decisions, and life situations.

Each advisor represents a different way of thinking and a different area of life. Some focus on logic and strategy, others on emotions, relationships, personal growth, health, finances, or values.

Hearing multiple viewpoints helps you explore ideas more thoroughly and reflect on situations from angles you might not have considered on your own.

Advisors are not meant to tell you what to do. Their purpose is to help you think more clearly, challenge assumptions, and consider different perspectives as you navigate your personal growth journey.

You may also rename advisors and customize certain aspects of the Tribe to make the experience more personal to you.

The goal of the Tribe is to create a space for reflection, learning, and thoughtful conversation through multiple perspectives.
