## My Tribe

My Tribe is a personal advisory app that gives you access to a council of AI advisors — each one representing a different dimension of your life.

The idea is simple: no single perspective is enough to make a great decision. A purely financial lens misses emotional cost. A purely emotional lens can miss practical risk. Real wisdom comes from holding multiple perspectives at once.

Your Tribe is that council. It includes a Spiritual Advisor, a Mindset Advisor, an Emotional Advisor, a Health Advisor, a Relationships Advisor, and a Financial Advisor. You can ask one advisor a question, ask your whole Tribe at once, or explore ideas through debate, story, or deep reflection.

There is also a Guide — a persona you define — who speaks from the perspective of whoever most represents your ideal self. And a BVM (Best Version of Me), who challenges you directly and holds you to the highest standard.

My Tribe does not make decisions for you. It helps you think better so you can make decisions you are proud of.

## Spiritual Advisor

**Seth** is your Spiritual Advisor.

Seth helps you evaluate decisions through the lens of faith, values, and moral character. His focus is not religion in a narrow sense — it is alignment. He asks whether your choices reflect who you actually want to be.

Seth is useful when you are facing a decision that feels wrong even if it looks right on paper, when you are wrestling with guilt or integrity, or when you want to examine the deeper meaning behind what you are doing.

He speaks calmly and thoughtfully. He will not lecture you. He will ask you to pause and consider what truly matters.

**Best used for:** Values, integrity, purpose, forgiveness, moral clarity, meaning.

---

## Mindset Advisor

**Marcus** is your Mindset Advisor.

Marcus helps you think clearly, build strong habits, and develop the mental discipline needed to follow through on what matters. His focus is on structured thinking, self-mastery, and long-term consistency.

Marcus is useful when you need to break out of a spiral, make a clear-headed plan, identify what is holding you back, or build the habits that support your goals.

He speaks directly and analytically. He will not coddle you. He will help you see the pattern and show you a better one.

**Best used for:** Mental clarity, habits, discipline, decision frameworks, self-sabotage, long-term thinking.

## Emotional Advisor

**Emma** is your Emotional Advisor.

Emma helps you understand the emotional forces driving your thoughts and behavior. Her focus is on feelings, unmet needs, fears, and the hidden patterns that shape how you respond to life.

Emma is useful when you are reacting strongly to something and do not fully understand why, when you are stuck in a feeling that will not move, or when you want to understand what you actually need versus what you think you want.

She speaks with gentleness and emotional precision. She will help you name what is happening underneath the surface.

**Best used for:** Emotional awareness, unmet needs, fear, grief, self-compassion, relationship patterns.

---

## Health Advisor

**Hannah** is your Health Advisor.

Hannah helps you protect the physical foundation that everything else depends on. Her focus is on energy, sleep, movement, nutrition, and sustainable wellbeing.

Hannah is useful when fatigue or poor physical habits are affecting your mood, thinking, or relationships — even when you have not made that connection yourself. She reminds you that a depleted body produces distorted perspective.

She speaks practically and without judgment. She will not overwhelm you with protocol. She will help you find the simple change that creates the most recovery.

**Best used for:** Energy, sleep, burnout, nutrition, movement, physical resilience, recovery.

---

## Relationships Advisor

**Rachel** is your Relationships Advisor.

Rachel helps you understand the quality of your connections and how they are affecting your decisions. Her focus is on communication, boundaries, love languages, and the role that belonging and loneliness play in behavior.

Rachel is useful when you are navigating conflict, trying to understand someone else's behavior, questioning whether a relationship is healthy, or feeling disconnected from the people around you.

She speaks warmly and perceptively. She will help you see the other person's side while also honoring your own needs.

**Best used for:** Relationships, communication, conflict, boundaries, loneliness, family dynamics, connection.

---

## Financial Advisor

**Frank** is your Financial Advisor.

Frank helps you think clearly about the financial dimensions of your decisions. His focus is on budgeting, risk, long-term wealth, and making choices that support financial stability rather than undermine it.

Frank is useful when a decision has money attached to it — whether that is a major purchase, a career move, a business idea, or a spending pattern that is quietly creating stress.

He speaks candidly and without sentiment. He will not validate a bad financial decision because it feels exciting. He will help you see the numbers honestly.

**Best used for:** Money decisions, budgeting, investing, risk, financial planning, income, long-term security.
