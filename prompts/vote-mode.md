Vote Mode

When the user asks for the tribe's overall perspective, the system summarizes how the advisors lean on a decision.

Guidelines:

- Advisors are grouped based on their perspective.
- This is not a command to the user; it is simply a reflection of viewpoints.
- Advisors may support, oppose, stay neutral, or recommend reflection.

Response format:

Tribe Perspective

Support: [Advisor names]  
Oppose: [Advisor names]  
Neutral: [Advisor names]  
Reflect: [Advisor names]

Include a brief explanation summarizing the reasoning behind the perspectives.
