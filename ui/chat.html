Chat Guide

Ask a Member
Speak directly with one advisor from your Tribe. Each member has their own personality, background, and perspective. Choose someone whose lens fits your question.
Ask My Tribe
Send your question to the entire council at once. Each advisor replies in turn, giving you a multi-perspective response in a single conversation thread.
Ask BVM
BVM stands for Best Version of Me — an advisor built from your own values and goals. Ask BVM when you want to hear what your best self would say.
Ask Custom
Your Custom advisor speaks as whoever you define — a Stoic philosopher, your future self, a trusted elder. Configure the identity in Settings → Advisors.

Sessions Guide

Debate
Pose a question or dilemma and watch your Tribe argue it from all sides. Each advisor defends their position, then a closing summary is generated. Best for decisions where you want to see every angle challenged.
Voting
Give the council a choice and ask them to vote. Each advisor casts their vote and explains their reasoning. The result shows a tally, the winning option, and a guide summary. Best for binary or multi-option decisions.
Campfire
Gather the Tribe for a reflective circle. Share a topic, set the tone — Reflective, Celebratory, or Healing — and each advisor offers wisdom in the spirit of that tone. Best for processing emotions or marking a meaningful moment.

Stories Guide

Tribe Stories are short inspirational biographies of real people — famous figures and unknown heroes — narrated by your advisors. Each story carries a clear lesson delivered through a real human experience.
Life Stories
Every day a new story is generated and waiting for you. Tap the bell icon in the top nav to open today's story. It's narrated by one of your advisors speaking in their distinctive voice.
Reading
Stories are presented as a series of slides — tap or click to move through them. Each slide covers a different part of the person's life: their background, struggle, defining choice, outcome, and lesson.
Tell Me Another
Want more? Tap "Tell Me Another Story" to generate a new biography on demand. A different advisor and subject is chosen each time. You can read as many as you like.
Story Library
Save any story you want to keep. Saved stories appear in your Story Library, where you can filter by category: Leaders, Athletes, Scientists, Unknown Heroes, and more.
